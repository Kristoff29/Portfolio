HISTORY

It all started in 2010, when Nirvana & Legacy Hotel was established by Russell Roosevelt, situated in San Diego. The hotel officially unlocked its door on October 20, 2010, and was regarded as the "Luxurious Hotel of the Southern Coast". During its early years, it already gained its well-deserving popularity. This sudden success brought success to the franchise, which resulted to gaining the attention of hospitality industry.

Up to this day, the company is continuously growing; inspiring life's most meaningful journeys in the most beaufitul destinations across globe.
